How we work

What we do

We are empowered to change the hospitality sector and support our customers in reaching their local market. We work with you and your business needs to help you tell your story and reach new audiences through great content, engaging copy, insights and reporting, and a strong relationship with you.

Step 1

We’ll analyse your business; we’ll look at your values, we’ll listen to your story and find the best way to tell it.

The first step will be to get to familiarise ourselves with your business. Your designated Community Manager will do an audit of your current online presence and look at what we can do to help and support you.

Step 2

We’ll roll out an online strategy just for you with objectives, content ideas and measurable results.

We’ll work on a digital strategy for building an audience by publishing, maintaining, and spreading frequent and consistent content that inspires, educates and entertains and that turns your fans into customers and your customers into advocates. Your digital strategy will allow us to discuss and set objectives and allows us to define your brand so we can begin working for you.

Step 3

We’ll get your pages set up, making sure your branding is consistent and you’re looking smart

Your designated Community Manager will ensure brand consistency on your pages. We’ll organise a photoshoot for you, shooting dishes and scenarios based on the objectives you have set us. We will seek your approval on our content ideas for the first few weeks as we get to know your brand.

Step 4

We’ll monitor your online channels and develop growth

Your designated Community Manager will work with our team of designers, writers and marketers to ensure we create content that speaks to your current customers and attracts the attention of new ones. We’ll post in a variety of formats from images to video to ensure engagement, to tell your story in a digital way and to drive people to your online bookings or front door.

Step 5

We’ll supervise your reputation to protect your brand

Your designated Community Manager will keep a close eye on reputation sites like Trip Advisor, Google and Yelp and will answer any reviews in a timely manner and authentically. Any negative reviews can be escalated for your attention to ensure you remain up to date. We’ll respond to positive reviews ensuring you get the most from them.

Step 6

We’ll keep you in the loop and deliver results

Your designated Community Manager will check in with you regularly to keep you up to date and to discuss any reactive content. We will share regular reporting with you so you can see what is happening and to show you the power of social.

Step 7

We’ll give you control

With our new app you will have control and visibility in your pocket. You will have access to a dashboard that provides overarching real-time visibility of content, results, reputation, joined-up communication between your teams and an open chat channel with your Community Manager. One place to see oversee all your online channels.
